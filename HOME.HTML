Early Years

Omar was born "Dave Omar Amin" On February 23rd 1990 in Toronto, Ontario to the proud parents of Azeez and Chandni Amin. Omar weighed in at 10 pounds and 3 1/2 ounces. He was abnormally large for a baby. He is the youngest out of his brother and sister.

Current Events

Omar has spent 13 years in the automotive industry, working his way up from a lot attendant/1st level apprentice mechanic, to a Service Manager for a dealership service department. Due to complications with with the worldwide pandemic COVID-19, Omar was laid off from his job. He then thought to himself to continue his education in the software programming field. He is currently enrolled at Trios College in the "Applications Developer" Program.

Career

Majority of Omar's career consists of the automotive industry however, he has had a short stint in acting and modelling, on and off, between 2015 and 2020. Omar was in the automotive industry from 2007 to 2018, and again in 2020. Within the years, he has held many positions from shop staff to management. Omar also had a two year stint in trucking between 2018 and 2020. He mainly held administrative positions in the mechanical aspect of the industry. Omar is now currently seeking a career in applications development.
